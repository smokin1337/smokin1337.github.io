Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

PeopleDrop respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the PeopleDrop app.

Information You Provide

PeopleDrop allows you to create and manage contact profiles that may include personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, company, job title, websites, and other details you choose to add.

This information is entered voluntarily by you and remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Contact Access

PeopleDrop may request access to your device’s contacts to enable sharing and saving contact information. This access is used only for app functionality and is never used to collect or store contacts without your action.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used solely to provide PeopleDrop’s core features, including:

- Creating and managing contact profiles
- Sharing contact information via QR codes, share sheets, or vCards
- Saving shared contact information when you choose to do so

Data Storage

PeopleDrop does not create user accounts and does not store your personal data on external servers. Your information remains on your device unless you choose to share it with others.

Data Sharing

PeopleDrop does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is only shared when you explicitly choose to share it using the app’s features.

Analytics and Tracking

PeopleDrop does not track users across apps or websites and does not sell user data. Any analytics used are limited to improving app performance and functionality.

Your Choices

You are always in control of what information you add, manage, and share within PeopleDrop. You can edit or delete your profiles at any time.
