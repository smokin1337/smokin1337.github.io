Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

PeopleDrop respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the PeopleDrop app.

Information You Provide

PeopleDrop allows you to create and manage contact profiles that may include personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, company, job title, websites, and other details you choose to add.

All information is entered voluntarily by you and remains stored locally on your device unless you choose to share it.

Camera Usage

PeopleDrop may request access to your device’s camera solely to allow you to take a profile photo. Camera access is optional and is not used for video recording or scanning. Profile photos remain on your device.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used only to provide the app’s core functionality, including:

  • Creating and managing contact profiles
  • Sharing contact information via QR codes, share sheets, or vCards

Data Storage

PeopleDrop does not create user accounts and does not store personal data on external servers. All profile information remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Data Sharing

PeopleDrop does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data is shared only when you explicitly choose to share it using the app’s features.

Analytics and Tracking

PeopleDrop does not track users across apps or websites and does not collect analytics data.

Your Choices

You are always in control of the information you add, edit, or delete within PeopleDrop. You can also revoke camera permissions at any time through iOS system settings.
