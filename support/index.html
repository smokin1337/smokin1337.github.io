Frequently Asked Questions

  • How many free questions do I get?
    3 AI questions per day on the free tier. The limit resets every 24 hours.
  • How do I cancel my subscription?
    Open iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → xplAIned → Cancel. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
  • How often is model data updated?
    The model database refreshes every day automatically. New models are typically added within 24 hours of their official release.
  • A model has wrong pricing or specs.
    Email us at the address above and we'll fix it the same day. Pricing data changes frequently and we appreciate the heads up.
  • How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
    Open xplAIned → tap the paywall → tap Restore. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID.
